ABOUT
I have lived in Riverton for 35 years. I raised my family here and have seen Riverton grow from a small community with a single grocery store to the city it is today. Over the years, I have been very involved in city issues including road expansion, water issues, public safety services and tax issues. I want to work with the residents regarding proposed expansion of Trax on 12600 South, maintaining open spaces for all residents to enjoy and control growth so Riverton maintains its small-town charm. I understand and am mindful of residents property tax concerns and want Riverton to be affordable for all those who call Riverton home. I want fair and equal treatment for all residents and will fight hard for District 5.
